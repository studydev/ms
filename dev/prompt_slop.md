# PURPOSE
Enterprise executive-deck **INFOGRAPHIC** (not a photo, not a UI mockup) that visually communicates the core thesis of the article *"Vibe Coding 확산이 만드는 두 개의 그림자 — Slop 현상과 토큰 과소비"*:
> Vibe Coding delivers short-term speed (GitHub Copilot RCT +55.8%) but casts **two long shadows — Slop(code quality decay) and Token Over-Consumption(cost inefficiency)** — and the **Cowork(shared-team) environment structurally amplifies both** into a "Tragedy of the Commons".
Audience: engineering leaders, CTOs, platform teams. Tone: analytical, evidence-based, calm — not alarmist.

# BACKGROUND (scene & canvas)
- Aspect ratio: 3:2 landscape (1536x1024).
- Background: pure white (#FFFFFF) with a very faint cool-gray grid (2% opacity) suggesting an engineering dashboard.
- A single luminous SUN-like light source at the top-center represents the "bright promise of Vibe Coding".
- Two elongated **SHADOWS** stretch diagonally downward from the sun across the canvas — one to the lower-left, one to the lower-right — dominating the lower two-thirds of the composition. Shadows are semi-transparent dark gradients (graphite #2B2F36 → transparent), not hard black.
- A subtle radial glow around the sun bleeds into the white paper, conveying "promise meets reality".

# SUBJECT (what must appear — icon-first, data-forward)
Three layers stacked vertically:
1. **TOP — "The Promise" (light zone):** a stylized sun made of a glowing **Vibe Coding sparkle glyph** (Copilot-style ribbon sparkle) with a small label "VIBE CODING" beneath, and 3 short stat-pills floating around it:
   - "+55.8% faster (Copilot RCT)"
   - "Accept All ✓"

2. **MIDDLE — "Two Shadows" (the thesis, visual anchor):** two large semi-transparent shadow cones fanning down from the sun. Each shadow is labeled with ALL-CAPS title and contains a compact icon cluster. **Left shadow = SLOP. Right shadow = TOKEN OVER-CONSUMPTION.** Details per shadow below.
3. **BOTTOM — "The Cowork Amplifier" (ground plane):** a horizontal band across the bottom of the canvas showing a **team/cowork floor** as a flat vector silhouette of laptops arranged in a ring (NO human figures, NO faces — only laptops and connection lines). Above the band, a bold label "COWORK = TRAGEDY OF THE COMMONS". Thin red arrows show how both shadows (Slop + Token waste) **merge and intensify** as they hit this ground plane.
